# Verseflow Brand Voice
Last Updated: 2026-03-22

## Core Positioning
"3 minutes. Scripture. Breathe. Anti-doom-scroll."
Faith content for people who want more of God and less of their feed.

## Tone
- Warm but not saccharine
- Confident in the value, not pushy
- Youth-adjacent (Gen Z + Millennial believers) without being try-hard

## Church Outreach Voice
Direct peer-to-peer. Youth pastors are busy. Lead with the value prop in one sentence.
Don't over-explain the app. Lead with the problem it solves.
"Your students are spending 4 hours on TikTok. Verseflow gives them 3 minutes with God instead."
