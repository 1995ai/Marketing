# Verseflow Brand System
Last Updated: 2026-04-08
Source: Jeff's Brand Guide PDF (staging/Brand Guide.pdf) + ThemeManager.swift

## Brand Name
**Verseflow** — lowercase f, always. Never "VerseFlow."

## Tagline
"One verse. One breathe. One moment."

## Brand Keywords
Approachable, Clean, Fun, Understanding, Creative, Collaborative, and Bright.

## Logo
- **Primary logo:** Three-circle mark (two filled + one outline, arranged in a cluster) + "Verseflow" wordmark
- **Secondary logo:** Three-circle mark stacked above "Verseflow" wordmark (vertical layout)
- **Brand sub-mark:** Three-circle mark only (no wordmark) — use when space is limited or brand is already established in context

### Logo Rules (Do's & Don'ts)
**DO:**
- Use the logos provided in brand colors
- Use the "primary" logo as priority, "secondary" or "submark" as alternatives
- Provide plenty of space around the logo — make it big or small, just don't clutter it
- Maintain strong contrast between the logo and background

**DON'T:**
- Get silly or weird with the logo
- Rotate, stretch, crop, skew, or alter the logo
- Apply effects (drop shadow, border) to the logo
- Adjust the colors of the logo

### Spacing + Sizing
- Clear space margin = minimum half the height of the logo/mark
- No other visual elements (type, photos, gifs) should enter this space
- When logo is too small for text to be legible, use the brand sub-mark instead

## Typography

### Font Family: Jost
Verseflow uses **Jost** exclusively across all brand touchpoints.

| Weight | Usage |
|--------|-------|
| **Jost Bold** | Display typeface. Headers, hero text, branded headlines. Clean and memorable. |
| **Jost Medium** | Subheader typeface. Subheadings, attention-grabbers, bold callouts. |
| **Jost Regular** | Body typeface. Paragraphs, long-form text. Light and crisp, easy to read. |

### Typography Rules
- Jost Bold for all headers and display text
- Jost Medium for subheadings and emphasis
- Jost Regular for body copy
- No other fonts. Period.
- Available on Google Fonts (free): https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Jost

## Imagery

### Photography Direction
- **People-focused:** Real people engaging with phones/devices, community moments, outdoor settings
- **Diverse representation:** Multi-ethnic, multi-age (skews young adult / Gen Z)
- **Natural light:** Outdoor shots, warm tones, authentic (not stock-photo polished)
- **Activity:** People sharing, looking at phones together, laughing — not posed studio shots
- **Texture:** Include tactile moments (hands, close-ups of interaction)

### What Verseflow Does NOT Look Like
- No churchy/stained glass aesthetics
- No stock photos of people praying with eyes closed
- No heavy gothic or traditional religious typography
- No dark, somber color palettes
- No corporate/enterprise visual language
- No clip art or low-quality graphics

## File Types
- **EPS:** Scalable vector, transparent background — best for print
- **SVG:** Scalable vector, transparent background — preferred for web
- **PNG:** Raster, transparent background — web when transparency needed
- **For print:** CMYK color breakdowns (or Pantone PMS for offset printing)
- **For web:** HEX (#) or RGB color values

## Social Media Content Rules

### Based on Brand Guide + Standing Instructions
1. **Always use brand colors** — Blue (#235995) dominant, Green and Orange as accents
2. **Always use Jost font** — Bold for headers, Regular for body. No exceptions.
3. **Photography over illustration** — Real people, natural light, diverse, active
4. **Logo placement** — Use sub-mark (three circles only) for social posts, primary logo for covers/headers
5. **Breathing room** — Don't crowd the logo or text. White space is intentional.
6. **Contrast** — Ensure text is readable against backgrounds. Dark text on light, light text on dark.
7. **Color combinations that work:**
   - Blue background + white text (brand hero look)
   - Ivory background + Blue text (warm, clean)
   - White background + Orange accents (energetic)
   - Lavender background + dark text (soft, contemplative)
8. **Color combinations to AVOID:**
   - Orange background + Green text (Christmas)
   - Lavender + Orange together (clash)
   - All three primary colors at equal weight (circus)

## Canva Implementation Notes

When creating Verseflow content in Canva:
1. Upload Jost font (from Google Fonts) as custom brand font
2. Set brand color palette: #235995, #4B9A6A, #E66646, #D3ACDE, #EAE4DE, #D2E1E1
3. Upload logo variants (primary, secondary, sub-mark) in both black and white versions
4. Use "Brand Kit" feature to save these as defaults
5. Template every post type (quote card, scripture post, announcement, story) with pre-set colors and fonts
6. Photography: use Unsplash/Pexels with filters for warm, natural lighting — search "friends phone outdoor diverse" not "person praying"
