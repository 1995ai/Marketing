## Data Quality Notes

**What was reliably findable:**
- Church names, denominations, cities, and websites: High confidence across all 42 entries. All verified against official church websites or denominational directories.
- Youth ministry presence: Confirmed via dedicated youth ministry pages on church websites for the majority of entries.

**What was harder to find:**
- General contact emails: Only ~10 of 42 churches had a publicly listed general email. Most churches use contact forms on their websites. This is noted per entry.
- Youth pastor/coordinator direct emails: Rarely publicly listed. Holy Name of Jesus Cathedral (Raleigh) was a notable exception with the youth director's email publicly available.
- Specific membership/attendance numbers: Rarely published. The Summit Church (~12 campuses), Elevation Church (~17K weekly), Mars Hill (~2,500 weekly), and West Market Street UMC (~1,700 members) were exceptions.

**Recommendation:** For churches without public emails, the outreach approach should be: (1) use website contact forms, (2) call the church office and ask for the youth minister/coordinator, or (3) send a physical introductory letter to the church office address.
