## Strategic Notes

1. **West Michigan Reformed density:** CRC and RCA churches dominate Grand Rapids/Holland — these tend to have well-staffed youth departments with seminary-trained directors.
2. **Holland infrastructure:** Particularly strong youth ministry infrastructure due to Hope College and Western Theological Seminary presence.
3. **Jeff Brown (Episcopal):** Single contact for 3 churches' joint GREY youth program at St. Mark's/St. Andrew's — high-leverage contact.
4. **Multi-campus opportunities:** Woodside Bible (14 locations), Kensington, Heritage Church, NorthRidge — single org decision reaches many students. Worth phone calls to unlock contacts.
5. **Recent hires 2024-2025:** Several churches hired new youth staff recently — new hires may be more receptive to trying new tools.
6. **Ward Church already in list:** Ward Church Northville is already in the existing target list — excluded from this expansion. Contact Mark Ordus noted for reference.
7. **S.T.A.G.E. program:** First Presbyterian Royal Oak runs ecumenical youth program across Presbyterian + Lutheran churches in Berkley/Royal Oak/Birmingham — potential multi-church adoption.
