## Strategic Notes

### West Michigan Reformed Cluster
The RCA and CRC churches in Holland, Zeeland, Grand Rapids suburbs, and Rockford form a natural denominational network. They share conferences, pastor networks, and word-of-mouth channels. Landing 2-3 early adopters here could create organic spread. **Three churches actively hiring youth directors** (Christ Memorial, Third Reformed, Providence CRC) — churches investing in youth staff are most receptive to youth ministry tools.

### Novi/Northville Priority
This is Matthew's relocation area. Brightmoor Christian and Orchard Grove are standouts — Brightmoor has 3 verified youth staff emails, Orchard Grove grew their program from 8 to 100 students in one year. Both are high-energy programs likely to adopt new tools.
