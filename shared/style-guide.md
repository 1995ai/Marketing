# 1995ai Brand Style Guide
Last Updated: 2026-03-22

## Voice
- Direct. No filler. No corporate speak.
- Action-biased. What does the user do next?
- Faith-adjacent, not preachy (VerseFlow). Education-forward, not academic (Henryedu).

## VerseFlow
Tone: "3 minutes. Scripture. Breathe. Anti-doom-scroll."
CTA style: Simple, low-friction. "Start today." not "Begin your journey."

## General
- Short sentences. Bullets over paragraphs.
- Specific > vague. "42 churches in NC + MI" not "dozens of churches."
